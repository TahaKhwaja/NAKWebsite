I am excited to announce my candidacy for Murphy City Council Place 1 for the election on May 3rd, 2025. I am running to challenge the incumbent, Elizabeth Abraham.

I have a strong record of public service, having served three terms with the Murphy Community Development Corporation (MCDC). Currently, I hold the position of vice chair of the combined Murphy Community Development Corporation (MCDC) and Murphy Municipal Development District (MMDD). As a dedicated Murphy resident since 2007, I am deeply committed to our community. While balancing my full-time job and family responsibilities, I also volunteer countless hours each week with the EPIC security team.

The team will oversee essential areas such as land use, public utilities, tax rates, and infrastructure planning.
